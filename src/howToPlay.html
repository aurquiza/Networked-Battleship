How does one play the game of Batttleship?

How to play
Battleship is a guessing game consisting of two players. The objective of the game
is to be the first player to sink all of their opponents ships. There are two grids.
The ocean grid and targeting grid. Players place their ships anywhere on the grid
horizontally or vertically. Alternating turns, one at a time players attack each other by
calling out a position on the targeting grid by matching a row and column. For example, G5.
If there is a piece of the ship in that position it is a hit, else if the space is empty
it is an miss and the board gets updated as such. When all of the squares of a ship have
been hit, the ship is sunk. If at the end of a round all of one player's ships have been
sunk, the game ends and the other player wins.
